Mac and Cheese in a Cheese Waffle Cone

Description

This is a fun way to serve the classic mac and cheese. Cheddar cheese is melted and formed into crisp waffle cone and mac and cheese is poured in. I made this for a few kids and they loved it...gotta love cheese though.

Ingredients

  • 5 ounces Cheddar Cheese, finely grated
  • 1 (7.25 ounce) package Macaroni and Cheese mix
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 ounce sliced pepperoni
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese onto the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until edges start to brown and cheese is bubbling, 5 to 10 minutes. Remove from oven and cool to room temperature.
  4. Cut melted cheese into squares and form into cones. Freeze cones for 5 minutes.
  5. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain and return pasta to pot. Mix milk, butter, pepperoni, salt, and cheese packet into pasta until evenly combined.
  6. Fill cheese cones with macaroni and cheese.
